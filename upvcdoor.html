UPVC DOORS

UPVC (Unplasticized Polyvinyl Chloride) doors are known for their durability, strength, and low maintenance. Unlike regular PVC, UPVC is more rigid and resistant to weathering, fading, and cracking, making it ideal for both residential and commercial use. These doors provide excellent insulation, helping to regulate indoor temperatures and reduce energy bills. UPVC doors are also highly secure, often reinforced with steel or aluminum. They require minimal upkeep, as they do not need painting and are easy to clean. Available in a variety of styles, UPVC doors offer a cost-effective, long-lasting, and eco-friendly solution for modern homes and buildings

UPVC Doors

  • 1. **Durability**: UPVC doors are resistant to weathering, fading, and cracking, ensuring a long lifespan.
  • 2. **Low Maintenance**: UPVC doors are easy to maintain, requiring only occasional cleaning to keep them looking new.
  • 3. **Energy Efficiency**: They offer excellent insulation against heat and cold, helping to reduce energy bills.
  • 4. **Security**: UPVC doors are often reinforced with steel or aluminum for added strength and security.
  • 5. **Environmentally Friendly**: They are recyclable and have a lower environmental impact compared to other materials like wood or metal
  • 6. **Aesthetic Flexibility**: Available in various colors, finishes, and designs to match different interior and exterior styles.
