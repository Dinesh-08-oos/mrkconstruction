Black M-Sand

M-Sand (Manufactured Sand) is a substitute for natural river sand, produced by crushing rocks (usually granite or basalt) into fine particles. It has become a popular choice in construction due to its uniformity in grain size, higher strength, and consistency compared to natural sand.M-Sand is primarily used for concrete production, plastering, flooring, and other construction applications that require high-quality sand. It is often preferred in high-performance concrete (HPC) and in locations with limited access to river sand.

Rough Black M-Sand

Rough Black M-Sand is composed of coarser grains, which enhance cement bonding, making it perfect for heavy-duty structural applications. It is ideal for concrete, mortar, and other construction needs that require maximum strength and durability.

  • Coarse Texture: Improves cement adhesion, creating a stronger bond.
  • High Strength: Excellent for demanding construction projects.
  • Durable: Can withstand high loads and stress.

Plastering Sand

Plastering Sand has a balanced grain size, providing improved workability and consistency in concrete mixes. This sand is ideal for plastering and other applications requiring smooth finishes and uniform texture.

  • Balanced Grain: Enhances mix workability and flowability.
  • Consistent Quality: Ensures reliable performance in various projects.
  • Versatile: Perfect for plastering, masonry, and general construction purposes.
